Founder / Photographer

Storytelling blurs the line between memory and myth.

Samidavinci is an Atlanta, Georgia based image-maker exploring the tension between brutalist structures and the human form. My practice focuses on crafting cinematic narratives that feel tactile, intimate, and timeless.

Over the last decade I've partnered with design houses, musicians, and global publications to translate emotion into striking visuals. Every shoot is built with intention—scouting light, sculpting texture, and honoring the subject's voice.
